A growing archive connecting botanical folk with shops and gardens.

From a local's perspective.



Petal Scout is an initiative to spread the garden growth and love to those who want to explore! Many greenhouses lack an online presence- instead of working with each gardener or shop owner creating that, we figured why not make a space that gives them all a home.

Users can search by city, and then see what botanical hot spots are in that area. Each specific spot will have a small insight page exploring the greenhouse a bit further from a writeup and photos from whoever wishes to be the petal scout of that location.
